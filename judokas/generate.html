Judoka Texture Generator

Generates the six cube-face PNGs for a judoka.
Customise the name/country below, then save each face to judokas/<Name>/.

1. Click "Save folder as ZIP" to download all 6 faces in one go, or use individual face buttons.
2. Unzip — you'll get a YourName/ folder. Move it into judokas/.
3. Add the name as a new line in judokas/list.
4. Reload the game — the name appears in the dropdown automatically.

You can also replace any PNG with your own artwork (photo, illustration, etc.).
Recommended size: 512×512 px.
